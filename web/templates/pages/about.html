What is this?

This is my personal site. Its for whatever I want to put up here. Tooling, information, etc

Why

Because I have a domain and I'd like to use it. Additionally there have been many cases where I wanted to share information and I had to send them a copy of the file, rather than linking to it on this site.

Also, I want somewhere to host tooling because I cannot always remember which sites have the good versions of a tool and I cannot rely on them always being there.

The design goals of this site are to be modular, flexible, and extendable. I should be able to put just about anything on this site with a clean structure and have it maintain continuity with the rest of the site

Additionally, I dislike front end web dev, and front end in general, but a website is the easiest way to make a nicer interface for my programs.
